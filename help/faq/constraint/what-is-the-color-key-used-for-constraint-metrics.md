---
question: 'What is the color key used for constraint metrics?'
---

- LOEUF (loss-of-function observed/expected upper bound fraction)

  - LOEUF < 0.33: red
  - 0.33 <= LOEUF < 0.66: orange
  - 0.66 <= LOEUF < 1: yellow

- pLI (ExAC)

  - pLI > 0.9: orange

- Missense Z

  - Missense Z > 3.09: orange

- Synonymous Z

  - Synonymous Z > 3.71: red
